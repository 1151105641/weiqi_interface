This is the help.
To Add a new item and save it into the All Item List = "Ctrl+S" or clicking on "Save" button.
When the task in All Item List that pass the review date it will show up in the Review List.
To set the review date to tomorrow = click on the "Tomorrow" button.
To set the review date to day after Tomorrow = click on the "DayAfTomorrow" button.
To add on 1 date to the current review date = click on the "Add1" button.
To show the link that are clickable = click on "Link Window" button.
To print the content of text = click on "Print" button.
To delete the content in the all items list = click on "Delete" button.
To show the help window = click on "Help" button.
